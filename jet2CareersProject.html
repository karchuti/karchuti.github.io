Jet2Careers Sign in/up redesign using UX/UI

Jet2Careers project

While job seeking, I came across Jet2Careers job portal and having just completed my courses I wanted to apply my skills to their under developed site.

Here I transformed the off-balance, lacking hierarchy and un-appealing sign in/up section on the page into a vibran, appealing, balanced sign in/up pop up that has proper structure and hierachy.
