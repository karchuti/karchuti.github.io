UX Project

UX Project Design Stages

Design Stagees for the UX project

What we designed:

Using the ISO 9241-210 Lifecyle, we redesigned an app which could scan items and show you their environmental, business, and social impact. We started by empathising with the users by breaking down the app, looking for points of friction, recording numbers of clicks, transition screens and noting down concerns.

Next came defining the problems, goals and a hypothesis which with we would use to check that the problems and goals were tackled.

After, we diverged to create sketches of solutions to the set problem having taken inspiration from current design trends.

We then converged using voting techniques like heatmapping and voting to decide on the best solutions for the problems.

Having decided on the best solutions, we created a plan for the prototype, breaking down the solutions into easily displayed tasks on a kanban board and dividing up the responsibilities.

Nearly finishing, we came to the testing for which we created test plans and instructions on how to perform the tests to result in unbiased or swayed result.

Finishing, we created plans on what actions to take depending on the results we received from the testing for the next iteration of the app.
