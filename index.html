Website coded from scratch

Dissertation Project

For my dissertation project, I meticulously developed a website from the ground up, focusing on accessibility, usability, and informativeness.

Adhering closely to the WCAG guidelines and employing best coding practices, the website boasts exceptional accessibility. It incorporates essential features such as descriptive alt text for images, a meticulously structured hierarchy facilitating keyboard navigation, and carefully chosen color contrasts to accommodate individuals with varying visual abilities.

Furthermore, I meticulously designed the layout, navigation, and interactive elements based on UX principles, ensuring a seamless and intuitive user experience.

The inspiration behind the website's informative nature stems from the observation of the lack of clear documentation and abundant data on Datacenters. Hence, my aim was to bridge this knowledge gap in the most user-friendly manner possible.

Hobbies & Experiences

SkyDiving

Skydiving is an exhilarating experience that defies gravity and pushes your boundaries. As I stood on the ground, the anticipation built, but it wasn't until I was up in the plane, feeling the temperature drop and hearing the wind whistle by, that the reality of what I was about to do truly set in. Nerves gripped me as I prepared to take the leap into the unknown. The initial freefall through thick clouds felt like being pelted with ice needles, but once we emerged, the breathtaking view and the sensation of gliding with the parachute made it all worth it. Despite the initial fear, the thrill of skydiving left me eager to do it again in my lifetime.

Caving

Despite my skepticism due to some claustrophobia, I ventured into the depths of a cave. The confined spaces initially made me uneasy, but as I delved deeper, I found myself immersed in the unique beauty and tranquility of the underground world. Overcoming my fear, I navigated through narrow passages and marveled at the intricate formations that time had sculpted. Though challenging, the experience of caving left me with a newfound appreciation for nature's hidden wonders.

Bouldering and Climbing

Despite my fear of falling, I find solace in the ascent. Bouldering and climbing offer a physical and mental challenge unlike any other. As I scale rock faces and navigate challenging routes, each move requires focus, determination, and trust in myself and my abilities. Despite the occasional slip or setback, the sense of accomplishment upon reaching the summit is unparalleled. Each climb pushes me to overcome my fears and test my limits, making the journey to the top all the more rewarding.

Cycling

Whether commuting to work, exploring scenic greenways, or tackling thrilling bike parks, cycling has become a beloved hobby of mine. The freedom of movement, the rush of wind against my face, and the ever-changing scenery make every ride an adventure. Whether navigating bustling city streets or winding country roads, cycling allows me to connect with my surroundings in a way that's both invigorating and meditative. With each pedal stroke, I feel a sense of liberation and joy that keeps me coming back for more.

Ziplining

With a love for speed and adventure, ziplining offers an exhilarating rush unlike any other. Soaring through the air at high speeds, suspended above breathtaking landscapes, I feel a sense of freedom and exhilaration that's hard to replicate. While the initial leap may be daunting, the adrenaline-fueled thrill of racing through the air quickly erases any doubts or fears. As I zip along the line, taking in panoramic views and feeling the wind rush past, I'm reminded of the sheer joy of living life on the edge.

Quary-Carting

Descending a quarry in a high-speed cart with no railings might seem daunting, but the adrenaline rush is incomparable. The initial drop filled me with a mix of excitement and trepidation, but as I gained speed and control, the fear melted away, replaced by pure exhilaration. Drifting around corners and navigating the rugged terrain was both challenging and immensely satisfying. Despite the heart-pounding moments, the thrill of quarry-carting left me with memories that I'll cherish for a lifetime.

Paintballing

Engaging in strategic combat with paintball guns provides an adrenaline-pumping experience unlike any other. The satisfaction of seeing paint splatter on opponents amidst intense skirmishes is truly exhilarating. While I enjoy the fast-paced action of paintballing, I find myself drawn to airsoft for its smaller masks, wider variety of guns, and tactical gameplay. Whether dodging paintballs or coordinating maneuvers with teammates, the thrill of simulated combat never fails to get my heart racing.

Airsoft

Among my favorite hobbies, airsoft combines physical activity with strategic gameplay for an immersive and thrilling experience. Running through diverse terrain, dodging incoming fire, and outmaneuvering opponents requires quick thinking and teamwork. Despite the sting of getting hit, the adrenaline rush of intense firefights and the satisfaction of executing successful maneuvers make every match unforgettable. With a wide array of guns and playstyles to choose from, airsoft offers endless opportunities for excitement and camaraderie.
